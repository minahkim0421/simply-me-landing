Linda is 8 years old with a passion for art and fashion! Linda can be found making toys and outfits for her dolls out of random materials, or singing. Following her mom’s footsteps, Linda can found helping others- sometimes putting too many eggs in one basket and often finds herself overthinking or doubting herself. Linda is learning it’s okay to make mistakes and to stay true to herself.

Be part of our journey.

One night, Raymonii was brainstorming in the computer lab thinking about her passion for helping others, community building, and how vital self love is- thus, the idea for toys that promote self esteem was born. Next, she assembled our team and began to bring this idea to life through in-person interviews with customers (customer discovery), sketches, story building and now our first prototype! Now, we are on Instagram, documenting and bringing you along this journey with us. We want to hear your story and are working to create a diverse dolls that celebrate your story.
